Avena Caliente (Oats and milk hot drink)

Avena caliente, an oatmeal and milk hot drink, is a nutritional beverage often served with breakfast as/with breakfast.

Ingredients

  • ▢ ½ cup rolled oats, [58 gr]
  • ▢ 4 cup water
  • ▢ ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ▢ 4 cup milk (whole or skim), (see tips)
  • ▢ 2 cinnamon sticks
  • ▢ 6 cloves
  • ▢ ¼ teaspoon nutmeg (freshly-grated)
  • ▢ 4 tablespoons brown sugar, (or more, to taste)

Directions

  1. Combine water, milk, salt, and oats and mix (in a blender) until the oatmeal is nearly dissolved.
  2. In a 3 quart [3 lt] pot mix with cinnamon, cloves, and nutmeg. Heat over medium heat, stirring constantly.
  3. Once it breaks the boil, lower the heat and simmer until the volume has reduced to ¾ of the original.
  4. Remove from the heat and stir in sugar to taste. Serve warm.
